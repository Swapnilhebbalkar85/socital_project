About Us

We have been providing high-quality vegetables since 1987. Our journey began as G Traders in the local market, and over time, we steadily grew and expanded our operations. Since 1996, we have been proudly supplying vegetables to AHUJA CATERERS. Additionally, we export vegetables through Goa government routes, which are managed and operated by the Goa State Horticulture Corporation Ltd (GSHL).
